# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters and HR professionals looking to hire for cybersecurity roles or internships.

## Product Purpose
To showcase the user's cybersecurity skills, record and share security writeups, and secure job/internship opportunities.

## Brand Personality
Clean, Minimal, Professional.

## Anti-references
- Overly colorful or animation-heavy sites that are hard to read.
- Developer-focused sites that lack intentional design.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity first:** Ensure the content (writeups and skills) is the focal point, not overshadowed by design.
- **Professional confidence:** Project a reliable, clean, and organized image suitable for HR and recruiters.
- **Intentionality:** Every design choice should serve readability and structure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast and large text for clear readability.
